Who We Are

Leading innovator in laser marking technology for the thermal insulation strip industry

High-Tech Enterprise Specializing in Laser Marking Solutions

KINGVAN Technology Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive high-tech enterprise integrating research and development, manufacturing, marketing, and services.

We specialize in providing advanced laser marking equipment specifically designed for the thermal insulation strip industry. Our solutions help manufacturers achieve precise, permanent marking for quality control and traceability.

With over 10 years of experience and more than 500 satisfied clients worldwide, we continue to innovate and deliver cutting-edge technology that meets international standards.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and cost-effective laser marking solutions that empower manufacturers to achieve excellence in product quality and traceability.

Our Vision

To become the global leader in laser marking technology for the thermal insulation industry, setting new standards for precision, automation, and customer service.

Our Values

Innovation, Quality, Integrity, and Customer Success guide everything we do. We build lasting partnerships through trust and performance.
